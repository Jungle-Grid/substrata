# Demo script

## Demo goal

Show how Substrata prepares a source-grounded ECCN review workup while keeping human approval central.

## Setup

Use a public or fictional datasheet, a seeded workspace, and a completed run if remote execution may be delayed. Keep the [Compliance boundaries](COMPLIANCE_BOUNDARIES.md) available for questions.

## 2-minute demo

1. Open the landing page: “Substrata prepares cited ECCN review memo drafts for human approval.”
2. Enter the workspace and open a completed review.
3. Show extracted facts, candidate review paths, and uncertainty flags.
4. Open Company History and explain that it is comparison context, not automatic precedent.
5. Open the memo draft and audit trail; end with required human approval.

## 5-minute demo

1. Landing page — explain the semiconductor review problem.
2. Workspace overview — show documents, reviews, and review queue.
3. New classification — select Local or Remote; providers remain internal details.
4. Upload a source package — explain source preservation.
5. Review detail — show processing status and evidence-backed paths.
6. Facts, company history, reviewer questions, memo, audit trail, and human conclusion.

## Full walkthrough

Follow the 5-minute path, then show the source package link, citations attached to facts, candidate-specific missing information, memo download, and reviewer disposition controls.

## What to say on each screen

- **Landing:** “We reduce time to a review-ready workup, not to a legal answer.”
- **Upload:** “Every review starts with a preserved source package.”
- **Facts:** “These are extracted technical facts tied to source snippets.”
- **Review paths:** “These are candidate review paths awaiting qualified review.”
- **Company history:** “Prior internal work informs consistency but does not determine a new result.”
- **Memo:** “This is a review-ready memo draft for a qualified reviewer.”
- **Audit:** “The workspace records what was generated, reviewed, and changed.”

## What not to overclaim

Do not say Substrata guarantees an outcome or replaces export counsel. Do not describe prototype AMD execution as a production deployment.

## If a run is loading or delayed

Explain that processing is asynchronous and the page intentionally suppresses incomplete conclusions. Switch to a completed review and show the same source-to-evidence workflow.
