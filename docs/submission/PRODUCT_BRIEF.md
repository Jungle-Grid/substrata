# Product brief

## What is Substrata?

Substrata is an evidence-backed ECCN review assistant for semiconductor and advanced hardware teams. It turns source material and company history into review-ready memo drafts for human approval.

## Who it is for

In-house trade compliance managers, compliance analysts, and qualified advisers reviewing technical hardware documentation.

## Before Substrata

Reviewers hunt through datasheets, old memos, spreadsheets, and inboxes; reconstruct facts; then prepare a new review record with limited visibility into prior reasoning.

## With Substrata

Teams create a source package, extract technical facts, inspect candidate review paths and uncertainty flags, compare company history, and move a cited memo draft through human review.

## Key capabilities

- Source package upload and document preservation
- Extracted technical facts with snippets
- Candidate review paths and reviewer questions
- Company history comparison
- Review-ready memo drafts
- Human review status and audit trail

## Why company history matters

Prior classifications, approved memos, product-family context, and reviewer notes often contain the practical context needed to make a new review consistent. See [Company history workflow](COMPANY_HISTORY_WORKFLOW.md).

## Why human review remains central

Substrata exposes evidence and uncertainty for qualified judgment. It does not issue a final classification.

## Example use case

A semiconductor team uploads an accelerator-card datasheet. Substrata extracts memory, interface, performance, and security facts; opens advanced-computing and security review paths; finds relevant prior accelerator records; and drafts a cited memo for reviewer signoff.

## Future vision

Make the review-ready workup the durable system of record for recurring hardware compliance decisions.
