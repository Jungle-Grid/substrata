# Compliance Scope

## Initial Focus

The initial scope is limited to semiconductor, electronics, and advanced hardware technical documents, especially product datasheets and similar engineering-facing specification documents.

## Product Role

Substrata is an ECCN review assistant. It helps users:

- extract relevant technical facts
- organize evidence
- compare extracted facts against regulatory logic
- draft internal review materials

It does not issue final legal advice or final export determinations.

## Human Review Disclaimer

Every classification output requires qualified human review. No system output may be treated as a final legal conclusion without expert review and organizational approval.

## Citation Requirements

Any suggested ECCN candidate must be tied to citations that can be reviewed by a human. Citations should identify:

- source title
- source location or section
- quoted or summarized basis
- why the citation matters to the suggestion

## Uncertainty Handling

The system must surface uncertainty explicitly rather than hide it. Examples:

- missing key parameters
- ambiguous language in the datasheet
- multiple plausible ECCN candidates
- low-confidence mapping between extracted specs and control language

## What Must Never Be Automated as Final Determination

- final legal classification approval
- legal sign-off on exportability
- automated regulatory submission
- unattended acceptance of a candidate ECCN into an official record
- silent suppression of uncertainty or conflicting evidence
