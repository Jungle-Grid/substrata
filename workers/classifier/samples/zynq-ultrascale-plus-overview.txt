DS891 Zynq UltraScale+ MPSoC Overview
AMD Zynq UltraScale+ MPSoC family overview.

The Zynq UltraScale+ MPSoC products integrate a feature-rich 64-bit quad-core or dual-core Arm Cortex-A53 and dual-core Arm Cortex-R5F processing subsystem with programmable logic.

Features:
- 10-bit 200KSPS ADC with up to 17 external inputs.
- Single Channel (Bit) write capability for all control registers include data output register, direction control.
- Four 10/100/1000 tri-speed Ethernet MAC.
- Two master and slave I2C interfaces.
- PCI Express compliant interface.
- Supports LVCMOS, LVDS, and SSTL.
- JTAG Boundary-Scan System Monitor in PL.
- 214 PS I/O; UART; CAN; USB 2.0; I2C; SPI; 32b GPIO.
- 1 or 2 lanes of DisplayPort (TX only) at 1.62Gb/s, 2.7Gb/s, or 5.4Gb/s.
- Supports secure and non-secure boot modes.
- AXI Coherency Extension instruction and data cache with ECC.
- 256-bit AES-GCM.
- SHA-3/384.
- 4096-bit RSA.
