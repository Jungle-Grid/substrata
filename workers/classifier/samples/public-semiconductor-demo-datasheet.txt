Asteria A112 Edge Accelerator
Public Demo Datasheet Excerpt

The Asteria A112 is a semiconductor accelerator designed for edge inference workloads.
Fabricated on a 7 nm process node.
Delivers up to 128 TOPS INT8 peak compute throughput.
Supports 112 Gbps PAM4 SerDes lanes for high-speed backplane and retimer connectivity.
A radiation-tolerant packaging option is available for selected aerospace and high-reliability deployments.
The device integrates high-bandwidth memory interfaces and PCIe Gen5 host connectivity.

This bundled sample is intended for Substrata local demo and validation workflows only.
