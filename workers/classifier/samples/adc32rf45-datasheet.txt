Texas Instruments ADC32RF45

ADC32RF45 Dual-Channel, 14-Bit, 3.0-GSPS analog-to-digital converter (ADC).

Key features:
- Dual-channel RF-sampling ADC with JESD204B serial interface.
- 4 Lanes Per Channel at 12.5 Gbps.
- Package: VQFN.
- Power consumption: 3.2 W/Ch.

Device description:
The ADC32RF45 is a dual-channel high-speed analog-to-digital converter for direct RF sampling and phased-array radar receivers.

Application examples:
Applications include phased-array radar and electronic warfare.
