# Roadmap

## Week 1 Validation MVP

Goal: validate demand and workflow pain quickly.

- build upload plus mock classification demo
- produce draft memos from sample datasheets
- support a concierge workflow behind the product
- run buyer and user interviews with compliance stakeholders
- test which evidence outputs matter most: extracted specs, citations, or memo

## Month 1 Pilot Product

Goal: get a small number of real pilot users through end-to-end internal reviews.

- secure document upload and storage
- persistent runs and memos
- reviewer status tracking
- improved extraction quality for common semiconductor datasheet patterns
- basic organization separation
- repeatable local or managed worker execution

## Month 3 Compliance Workflow Product

Goal: move from demo utility to daily internal workflow software.

- richer review state transitions
- stronger audit timeline and reviewer attribution
- configurable regulatory logic versions
- better memo editing and export
- internal evidence linking from spec to citation to memo paragraph
- first pilot integrations with document systems or PLM-adjacent workflows

## Long-Term Platform Vision

Substrata becomes a compliance evidence and review platform for technical trade controls:

- versioned regulatory logic and model traces
- broader coverage across hardware product families
- reproducible job execution on Jungle Grid
- enterprise-grade permissions, retention, and approvals
- support for adjacent compliance tasks where technical-document reasoning matters
