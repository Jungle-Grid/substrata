Why now

The workflow has crossed an inflection point

Hardware complexity is rising faster than compliance teams can add reviewers.

01

Technical products are harder to review

Advanced compute, mixed-function devices, security features, and variants create more facts and more possible review paths.

02

Institutional knowledge is trapped

Prior decisions exist, but they are difficult to find, compare, and reuse with the provenance reviewers need.

03

AI can structure the work—now

Modern open models can extract technical evidence at scale when paired with deterministic controls and human approval.
